Movie Review: Avengers

Release Year: 2012

Movie Details

  • Director: Joss Whedon
  • Genre: Action, Sci-Fi
  • Runtime: 143 minutes
  • Rating: 4.8/5

Plot Summary

The Avengers, a team of remarkable superheroes, including Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye, are brought together by S.H.I.E.L.D. to save the world from the powerful and villainous Loki. Loki plans to use the Tesseract, a cosmic artifact, to conquer Earth. As the Avengers assemble, they must put aside their differences and work together to thwart Loki's destructive plans and save humanity.
